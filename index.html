Bruno Pinheiro

Product Designer @ McGowans Print

I started my career in 2001, after kick off my Graphic Design course, creating web pages using HTML, Flash, Photoshop and PHP. In 2004 I felt in love for 3D Modeling and Animation, so I did a Post-Graduation on this field and began working as a 3D Modeler at an Educational Company. From 2011 to 2014 I was working as 3D Environment Artist for a Gaming Company. In 2014 I opened my own company, started it as a 3D school, changed into a 3D Animation and Design Studio, and finally in a Virtual Reality Studio. In 2017 I decided to learn more about Business and took an MBA. Studying again, I noticed that I wanted more and it was the time to close the company and go beyond, so I came to Ireland. Now I am working as a Product Designer in a printing company, developing and executing 3D Printed marketing projects for Coca Cola, Guinness, Tayto, etc. In the meantime, I’m learning new technologies like Augmented Reality and taking a Bootcamp in UX design at Talent Garden.
